Tetris Game Rules

Objective

The objective of Tetris is to manipulate falling tetrominoes (shapes made up of four square blocks) to create complete horizontal lines without any gaps. When a line is complete, it disappears, and the player earns points.

Controls

  • Left Arrow: Move the tetromino left
  • Right Arrow: Move the tetromino right
  • Up Arrow: Rotate the tetromino
  • Down Arrow: Accelerate the tetromino's descent

Scoring

The player earns points for each line cleared. The number of points depends on the number of lines cleared simultaneously.

  • 1 Line: 10 points
  • 5 Lines (Tetris): 50 points, move to next level

Leveling Up

As the player scores points, they advance to higher levels. Each level increases the tetromino's falling speed, making the game more challenging.

Game Over

The game ends when the stack of tetrominoes reaches the top of the playfield, and there's no room to place new tetrominoes.
